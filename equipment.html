Equipment Management

A unified, audit‑ready system for tracking equipment, inspections, maintenance, certifications, and operational readiness across the entire fleet.

What the Equipment Module Does

Driv‑en’s Equipment Module centralizes every equipment‑related workflow into one dependable system. From inspections to maintenance to operator qualifications, the module ensures your fleet stays compliant, safe, and ready for the field.

Key Features

  • Equipment inventory with status, location, and readiness tracking
  • Work orders for repairs, maintenance, and corrective actions
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling with automated reminders
  • Equipment inspections with configurable checklists
  • Operator qualifications and certification tracking
  • Fuel logs, usage logs, and hour‑meter tracking
  • GPS and location reporting (optional integration)
  • Rented equipment tracking with vendor responsibility and return dates
  • Document storage for manuals, certifications, and maintenance records
  • Dashboards for readiness, downtime, and upcoming maintenance

How It Works

The Equipment Module is built on Driv‑en’s fault‑tolerant architecture with autosave, server‑side validation, and clean workflows. Every action is logged in the Activity and Timeline system for full auditability.
