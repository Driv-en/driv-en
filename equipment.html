Equipment Module

The Equipment Module centralizes inspections, maintenance, readiness, documents, and rental tracking into one unified system. Built for real-world field operations.

Module Overview

The Equipment Module provides a complete lifecycle view of every asset — from inspections and maintenance to transfers, documents, and GPS tracking. It is designed to keep equipment safe, compliant, and ready for deployment.

Key Features

  • Equipment Inventory with categories, status, and readiness
  • Work Orders for repairs, maintenance, and service tasks
  • Preventative Maintenance scheduling and tracking
  • Equipment Transfers between locations, projects, or crews
  • Inspection workflows with automated status updates
  • Fuel Logs for consumption and cost tracking
  • GPS Tracking integration for location visibility
  • Rented Equipment tracking with vendor responsibility
  • Operator Qualifications (OQ) validation
  • Equipment Documents (manuals, certifications, photos)
  • Dashboards for readiness, risk, rentals, and inspections

Automated Status Updates

Equipment status updates automatically based on inspection results, work orders, and maintenance activity. Statuses include Active, Needs Repair, and Out of Service.

Rented Equipment Tracking

Track all rented equipment company-wide, including vendor responsibility, agreements, condition, calibration, and return dates. Overdue rentals are automatically flagged.
