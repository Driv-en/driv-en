Equipment Management Module

Equipment Management Module Overview

The Equipment Management Module centralizes inspections, maintenance, certifications, readiness, and operational tracking for all heavy equipment. It is engineered for clarity, auditability, and real‑world field performance.

Included Features

  • Equipment Inventory
  • Work Orders
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Equipment Transfers
  • Inspections
  • Fuel Logs
  • GPS Tracking
  • Rented Equipment Tracking
  • Operator Qualifications (OQs)
  • Equipment Documents
  • Dashboards
  • Module Settings & Toggles

Operational Purpose

Driv‑en’s Equipment Management Module ensures every asset is accounted for, compliant, and ready for deployment. It provides supervisors, mechanics, and operators with a unified workflow that reduces downtime, improves safety, and maintains audit‑ready documentation.
