Equipment Management

The Equipment Module provides a complete operational framework for tracking, maintaining, and managing all company equipment. This includes inventory, inspections, work orders, preventative maintenance, rentals, transfers, GPS tracking, operator qualifications, and documentation.

Core Features

  • Equipment Inventory
  • Work Orders
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Equipment Transfers
  • Inspections
  • Fuel Logs
  • GPS Tracking
  • Rented Equipment Tracking
  • Operator Qualifications (OQs)
  • Equipment Documents
  • Dashboards & Reporting
  • Module Settings & Toggles

Equipment Inventory

Maintain a complete list of all equipment with details such as category, type, serial number, status, location, and assigned operator or project.

Work Orders

Create and manage work orders for repairs, maintenance, inspections, and field requests. Includes priority, category, photos, attachments, and assignment workflows.

Preventative Maintenance

Track maintenance schedules, intervals, and service history to ensure equipment remains safe, reliable, and compliant.

Inspections

Perform and document inspections with customizable checklists, photos, signatures, and automated status updates.

Rented Equipment

Track all rented equipment, including vendor details, rental agreements, condition, calibration requirements, and return dates.

Operator Qualifications

Manage operator certifications, expiration dates, document uploads, and integration with Equipment, Safety Training, and Employee modules.

Dashboards

View real-time equipment status, availability, risk levels, overdue rentals, inspections, and repairs.
