Driv-en Modules

Driv-en is built as a modular operational platform. Each module is designed to solve a specific operational problem while integrating seamlessly with the rest of the system. Below is an overview of the core modules available in Version 1.0.

Equipment Module

Manage all company equipment including inventory, maintenance, inspections, rentals, GPS tracking, and operator qualifications.

  • Inventory & Categorization
  • Work Orders & Repairs
  • Preventative Maintenance
  • Inspections & Checklists
  • Fuel Logs
  • GPS Tracking
  • Rented Equipment
  • Operator Qualifications
  • Dashboards & Reporting

Safety Module

Centralize all safety operations including training, certifications, meetings, incidents, JSAs, and compliance documentation.

  • Training & Certifications
  • Safety Meetings
  • Incident Reporting
  • Job Safety Analysis (JSA)
  • Compliance Tracking
  • Safety Documents

Project Module

Manage daily reporting, tasks, materials, photos, documentation, and project oversight from field to office.

  • Daily Reports
  • Tasks & Assignments
  • Material Tracking
  • Project Photos
  • Documentation
  • Project Dashboards

Pipeline Module

Track joints, heat numbers, weld logs, inspections, and traceability workflows for pipeline construction and maintenance.

  • Joint Tracking
  • Heat Numbers
  • Weld Logs
  • Pipeline Inspections
  • Traceability Documentation

Developers Module

Standardize developer onboarding, expectations, workflows, and evaluations to ensure consistent engineering output.

  • Developer Expectations
  • Onboarding Requirements
  • Development Workflow
  • Evaluation Criteria
  • Documentation Standards
