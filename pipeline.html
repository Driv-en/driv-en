Pipeline Management

A purpose‑built system for pipeline construction, integrity, compliance, and field execution. Driv‑en unifies every pipeline workflow into one structured, audit‑ready module.

What the Pipeline Module Does

Driv‑en’s Pipeline Module centralizes construction, inspection, integrity, and regulatory workflows into a single, dependable system. From pipe tally to hydrotest to as‑builts, the module ensures accuracy, traceability, and compliance across the entire pipeline lifecycle.

Key Features

  • Pipeline construction workflows with project‑level configuration
  • Pipe Tally with heat numbers, joint numbers, and material traceability
  • Weld Mapping with welder IDs, procedures, and NDE linkage
  • Coating records with defect tracking and repair documentation
  • NDE tracking (RT, UT, MT, PT) with acceptance criteria
  • Hydrotest documentation with pressures, charts, and approvals
  • As‑Built documentation with GPS, stationing, and weld logs
  • Pipeline Regulatory Reporting (PHMSA‑aligned)
  • ILI / Pigging Portfolio View for integrity programs
  • Pipeline Integrity Dashboard with compliance indicators
  • Material receipts and heat traceability
  • Activity logs and audit‑ready timelines

How It Works

The Pipeline Module is built on Driv‑en’s fault‑tolerant architecture with autosave, server‑side validation, and structured workflows. Every weld, joint, test, and inspection is logged in the Activity and Timeline system, ensuring full traceability from ditch to ditch.
