Pipeline Module

The Pipeline Module provides complete traceability, documentation, and workflow management for pipeline construction, maintenance, and integrity operations. Every joint, weld, heat number, inspection, and test is captured in a structured, audit-ready format.

Pipe Tally

Track every joint, heat number, coating type, wall thickness, grade, and material attribute required for pipeline traceability.

  • Joint Number
  • Heat Number
  • Pipe Size & Wall Thickness
  • Grade & Specification
  • Coating Type
  • Location / Stationing
  • Material Status

Weld Logs

Document every weld with welder ID, procedure, pass count, defects, repairs, and inspection results.

  • Welder ID & Qualifications
  • WPS / PQR
  • Root, Hot, Fill, Cap
  • Defects & Repairs
  • NDE Results
  • Acceptance / Rejection

NDE (Non-Destructive Examination)

Track all inspection methods including X-ray, UT, MT, PT, and visual inspections.

  • Inspection Type
  • Inspector ID
  • Results & Findings
  • Repair Requirements
  • Final Acceptance

Coating Records

Document coating applications, repairs, environmental conditions, and holiday testing.

  • Coating Type
  • Surface Prep
  • Environmental Conditions
  • Holiday Test Results
  • Repair Notes

Hydrotest

Capture all hydrotest data including pressures, durations, charts, and acceptance criteria.

  • Test Section
  • Pressure & Duration
  • Charts & Graphs
  • Test Medium
  • Acceptance Criteria

As-Built Documentation

Generate complete as-built packages including weld maps, tally sheets, NDE reports, coating logs, and hydrotest documentation.
