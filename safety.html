Safety Module

The Safety Module provides complete safety management — meetings, inspections, incidents, SDS, JSA/JHA, training, and compliance — all in one unified system.

Module Overview

Driv‑en’s Safety Module is built for real‑world field operations. It centralizes safety documentation, training, inspections, incidents, and compliance tracking into a single, audit‑ready workflow.

Core Workflows

  • Safety Meetings: Tailgate meetings, attendance, topics, signatures, and documentation.
  • Safety Inspections: Checklists, findings, corrective actions, and follow‑up tracking.
  • Incident Reporting: Near misses, recordables, first aid, property damage, and investigations.
  • OSHA Recordkeeping: 300, 300A, 301 logs and automated classification support.
  • JSA/JHA: Job safety analysis creation, hazards, controls, and approvals.
  • SDS Management: Chemical inventory, SDS library, hazard communication compliance.
  • Training & Certifications: Expiration tracking, requirements, and employee qualification status.
  • Traffic Control Plans: Uploads, approvals, and project‑specific requirements.
  • Safety Equipment: PPE, gas monitors, calibration, bump tests, and readiness.
  • Employee Handbook: Upload, distribution, and acknowledgment tracking.
  • Site‑Specific Safety Plans: Project‑level safety documentation and requirements.

Incident Management

The Safety Module supports full incident workflows — from initial report to investigation, root cause analysis, corrective actions, and OSHA classification.

Compliance & Reporting

Driv‑en automatically compiles safety metrics, training status, incident rates, and compliance dashboards for company‑wide visibility and audit readiness.
