Safety Management

A complete, audit‑ready safety system built for real‑world field operations. Driv‑en centralizes safety programs, inspections, training, incidents, and compliance into one dependable module.

What the Safety Module Does

Driv‑en’s Safety Module unifies every safety workflow into a single, structured, OSHA‑defensible system. From safety manuals to incident investigations, the module ensures clarity, accountability, and compliance across the entire organization.

Key Features

  • Safety Manual and Employee Handbook storage
  • SDS / HazCom program with searchable chemical index
  • Safety Meetings with attendance tracking
  • Safety Inspections with configurable checklists
  • Safety Observations and Unsafe Acts reporting
  • Accident / Incident Investigations with RCA workflow
  • OSHA Recordkeeping (300, 300A, 301)
  • Near Miss reporting and trending
  • JSA / JHA creation, storage, and approvals
  • Safety Dashboard with TRIR, LTIR, and other KPIs
  • PPE Inventory and assignment tracking
  • Site‑Specific Safety Plan (SSSP) builder
  • Training & Certifications with expiration alerts
  • Rented Safety Equipment tracking
  • Tool Safety integration with automatic safety flags

How It Works

The Safety Module is built on Driv‑en’s fault‑tolerant architecture with autosave, server‑side validation, and clean, audit‑ready workflows. Every action is logged in the Activity and Timeline system, ensuring full traceability for compliance and investigations.
