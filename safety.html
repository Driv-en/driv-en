Safety Management Module

Safety Management Module Overview

The Safety Management Module centralizes training, certifications, meetings, incidents, JSAs, inspections, and compliance documentation into one unified workflow. It is engineered for operational clarity, OSHA defensibility, and real‑world field performance.

Included Features

  • Training Records
  • Certifications & Renewals
  • Safety Meetings
  • Incident Reporting
  • Job Safety Analyses (JSAs)
  • Safety Inspections
  • Corrective Actions
  • Safety Documents
  • SDS / HazCom
  • Dashboards
  • Module Settings & Toggles

Operational Purpose

Driv‑en’s Safety Management Module ensures every worker, crew, and project maintains compliance, readiness, and documentation integrity. It provides supervisors and safety teams with a unified workflow that reduces risk, improves communication, and maintains audit‑ready safety records across the organization.
